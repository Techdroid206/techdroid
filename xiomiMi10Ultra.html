Xiomi Mi 10 Ultra

Xiaomi, which is pretty well known for it's budget friendly mobile, has come up with a flagship model this time.
Coming to box, they give us an instruction manual, Sim ejector tool, USB C cable, head phone jack c extension cable and a massive 120w charger along with mobile (probably the most powerful charger you can get along with a smart phone right now).

Alright, coming to the phone, the term "ultra" it is really justified? Let's check it out.
The spec sheet itself makes you go 'woah'.

  • 6.67 inch 120hz display
  • Quad-cam set up with dual 8k video
  • 120x zoom
  • 4500 mAh battery
  • 120w charging
  • 50w wireless charging(two times more powerful as Galaxy S 20 ultra's wired charging)
  • QSD 865 chipset
  • 8-16 gigs of ram (LP DDR 5, literally the fastest)
  • 128 - 512 gigs of storage
  • liquid cooling
  • six stacks of graphite
  • graphine

The spec sheet is really on an ultra level. Oh wait, then the phone might be too costly? Well we're talking about MI here, they've been budget friendly right from the start - it just starts from 760USD and yeah you don't need to double check, iam being totally serious here. So practically it costs less than base Galaxy S 20.
With the cameras coming down almost to the half way(w.r.t mobile), it gives us a beastly look. So fingers touch your lens a lot.
It loads games and apps quicker than any mobile out there, haptics and dual speaker set up are very good. It runs on Xiaomi MIUI 12. It provides us new fonts, new way of organizing our settings menu, new animations.

Alright, coming to the main menu, the camera. Want to know something? This is the highest rated camera in the world right now. But how good it is actually? We've got

Main camera is just 48MP? Can it take decent pictures? Well it can take exceptionally good pictures. The colours are so natural and detailed with almost 0 noise. The dynamic range is so good that it even classes out Galaxy S20 Ultra. The night mode takes some good clicks as well. We can actually take pictures with same quality even when we zoom in 4 times. It comes with a front and back future which allows us to use any two combinations (even both front and back camera) at the same time.it has got 8k recording mode. So it is the best camera phone we've got out there? Well it lacks a thing though, auto focus. When you move around multiple objects which resembles with one another, it has some hard time on doing the job. But over all the package we get, it's definitely one of the best camera featured smart phone out there.
And this guy charges upto 50% in just 6 mins.

Do we recommend it? - well before you start searching it, I would like to let you know that this is available only in China, if you want one, then you should probably import it. But for money, it definitely serves the best.
