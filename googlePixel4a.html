Google Pixel 4A

Google Pixel 4A, it is the most impressive phone Google has ever produced. What comes in the box? Mobile itself with a C cable and 18W charger(with some instructions manual which you probably won’t be looking at). It just costs 349 USD, impressive right?

You might be wondering that this looks pretty simple. Well, Google did exactly that. They just put the word “SIMPLE” into action. A single 12 MP camera? Does it take any good pictures compared to the new range 48 pr 64MP quad-cam set up? Here is the thing, forget about the mid-range mobiles, Google Pixel 4A takes much better pictures than the modern-day flagship. The image process is so good that the colour and crispness this time even exceeded much more than Pixel 3A. It has a pretty robust portrait mode which grabs the details efficiently. If you put on a tripod stand and take night mode shots, you will get the best asteroid photo one has ever taken on a mobile which is even better than Huawei mobile.

Pheww, Camera does exceptionally well, which is expected from Google anyway, but what about the other stuff? 4A comes with the same plastic material as 3A. The impressive thing is that Google stuck to the original plastic texture and embraced it without actually trying to make it look glassy, which is a pretty gutsy move. Unlike modern-day fingerprint sensor, it still comes with a fingerprint sensor on its back which isn’t much of a problem but does matter a little. Now, Bessels – Google finally tried removing their extra-large bessels from their previous generation and came up with a single punch hole camera. Size of this guy is remarkably compact and anyone, who doesn’t love ultra big mobiles which don’t fit properly in their pockets, would love Google Pixel 4A.

And great news, Google is still going with 3.5mm headphone jack (yayy, right?). Also, I will not hesitate to tell you that this is the most intelligent and advanced Google assistant right now in the market. It can generate auto-captions on any platform you use on this device, which is kind of interesting because language isn’t a barrier here.
Google has offered three years of Android updates on this mobile which is pretty good because a lot of mid-rangers do that to a maximum of 2 or sometimes 1. So, we get Android 13 in this tiny little guy when the time comes (only if you can keep your mobile in one piece until then).

Well, time to discuss a few things which it lacks – chipset, it comes with 730G QSD. It isn’t exactly a slow one, but compared to the modern-day mid-range chipsets like QSD 765G or A13B, it indeed is a drawback. It has a 60hz refresh rate, because of this low refresh rate it does feel a little slow compared to Nord. By the way, you get a few advantages here, Youtube premium for free, 100 gigs of Google drive, Play pass, which is handy, especially the 100gigs of storage.

Do we recommend it? – see, you might be confused with a 400 USD mobile with this one, I would say they are entirely different species. If you are on a tighter budget go for Pixel 4A without a second thought because you are saving some 50-100 USD on this one with original google android which is way better than any other Android OS out there. So, I guess you got your answer.

Happy Shopping!
