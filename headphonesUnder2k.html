Top 10 headphones under 2000

Are you on a budget and don't want to spend too much on a headphone, but want some good sound quality in limited budget? Don't worry we'll help you out.
Here we'll try briefing each headphone's major specs and rate it on 3 factors - sound quality, bass, build

1. infinity(JBL) glide 510

infinity is a sub brand of jbl, so I don't exactly need to say how good the sound quality will be. It comes with a nice matte finish with 72 hours of playback time with double equalizer(one for deep base and one for normal) and comes with 36mm driver. Only draw back I wound say is it doesn't support aux connection instead it comes with Bluetooth 5.0

Sound - 9/10
Bass - 9/10
Build - 7.5/10

2. philips SHB3075 BL

Philips has been in electronics field for so long. This comes with a unique design. It comes with 32mm drivers, 5.0 Bluetooth and again no, aux cable with this guy. And playback time is just 12 hours.

Sound - 9/10
Bass - 8/10
Build - 7.5/10

3. HP pavilion Bluetooth headset 600

It comes with a pretty simple design. It comes with a 4.0 Bluetooth connectivity, 12 hours of play back time,40 mm drivers.best part being it comes with a headphone jack, so it isn't useless when battery drains out.

Sound - 8.5/10
Bass - 7.5/10
Build - 7.5/10

4. Lenovo HD100 wireless Bluetooth

This a bulky headphone from Lenovo, it comes with a massive 40mm driver, 5.0 Bluetooth, 20 hours of play back time.also it comes with a headphone jack(hell yeah). If you're a fan of bulky headphones go for this.

Sound - 9/10
Bass - 9/10
Build - 9/10

5. Motorola escape 200

It comes with 40mm drivers, 15 hours of playback time. It comes with an aux cable

Sound - 8.5/10
Bass - 9/10
Build - 8.5/10

6. boat rockers 510

It is a aggressive looking ear phone which better suits gamers comes with aux cable, 10 hours of play back time, Bluetooth 5.0 and 50 mm drivers, so basically the clarity you get from this guy is pretty amazing.

Sound - 9/10
Bass - 9.5/10
Build - 9/10

7. boat rockers 450 wireless

It is more classy and comes with connectivity of 4.0 Bluetooth, aux cable port, 8 hours of play back time ,40 mm drivers

Sound - 8/10
Bass - 8/10
Build - 7.5/10

8. Zebronics Zeb Duke bluetooth

It is a stylish headphone coming with leds on buds with 40 mm drivers, 30 hours of play back time

Sound - 9.5/10
Bass - 9/10
Build - 9/10

9. Panasonic RP HF 410 BGCG

It comes with 30mm driver, 24 hours of playback time, 5.0 Bluetooth, with aux cable. It is a bit expensive though

Sound - 8.5/10
Bass - 7/10
Build - 7/10

10. Syska HSB3000 sound pro

It comes with 40mm drivers, aux cable, 10 hours of playback time, 5.0 Bluetooth connectivity

Sound - 8/10
Bass - 9/10
Build - 7.5/10

Alright, these are the best picks according to us so far.
Which one do we recommend? - that's a tough one, but I would go with one among infinity or boat rockers 510 or zebronics because all of them come under 1800 which is really budget friendly and with some amazing sound quality.

Happy Shopping!
