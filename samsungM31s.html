Samsung M31s

M 31s has been Samsung's best M series model. It comes with a decent plastic back. This guy has an whooping 6000mAh battery which gives us an 8 hour plus screen on time with 25W charger included(charges in 45-50 mins). This on screen time can also be increased if you opt for optimization given inside the phone.

Coming to screen it has got 60hz super amoled display.Well we're talking about Samsung here, these guys will always give their best to provide good displays with decent colours.
Has got a single speaker beneath. But don't mind, they still keep the 3.5mm crown safe so we can put on headphones avoiding the world.

Camera -

12MP ultra wide
5MP depth
5MP macro

Alright, the budget - 19k roughly, it definitely takes some smooth pictures.It provides some good dynamic range and also stability using EIS. One thing which definitely stands out for the price point is shutter speed, it's absolutely point on. We can record in 4k With 30fps

It definitely is a good mid range phone, but in india it comes with exynos 9611 chip. I mean why Samsung, Just why?
We've literally had the best screen in that given budget, best battery, decent looking punch hole camera, nice skin, nice feeling one UI, what's lacking is the big guy powerful processor. So this thing can't be powerful for extensive gaming.
Other than that I don't think there's anything else to hate this phone unless you don't like Samsung in particular.

Do we recommend it? - Well if you're someone who's not into intense gaming, but need mobile for day to day activities, this phone is just for you.
