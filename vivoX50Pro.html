Vivo X50 Pro

Smartphone companies these days either stick to embracing new technology coming into the market or develop one new feature on their own. Vivo recently introduced something new and cool, which probably will be embraced by others in the coming future. Older cool features which Vivo was the first to introduce were – infinite display, first in-display fingerprint sensor, the first pop up camera. Alright, these indeed bought something revolutionary among smartphones, especially the in-display fingerprint scanner. But what did Vivo bring this time which they claim to be the future of cameras? Let’s see about that.

It has got 6.56” 1080p AMOLED display, 90hz refresh rate, curved display edges, punch hole camera at the top left. Alright, you get these features in many smartphones these days, what makes this phone so special? Camera system. AI Quad camera set up at the back is in its own league – 48MP main cam, 2x zoom, 8MP ultra-wide, 5x optical zoom. It used the gimbal system. Well, smartphones these days take shots using OIS or AI in built-in mobile which indeed gives pictures decent stability. But Vivo has gone a step far and introduced this stabilized control through hardware.

This gimbal system camera makes sure that the main camera moves as little it can to get a stable model with maximum accuracy. What makes it different from OIS, well an OIS camera actually pinpoints an object and turns left and right or top and bottom, but this gimbal system does add one more feature of tilting front and back (which accounts for 3 times more than what a normal camera can do) which adds extra stability and all those micro-movements made by hand are gone, which makes us feel like, yeah we don’t need any balancer to take candid shots anymore.


Video shots on mobiles till this day used EIS which is basically framing out the borders, keeping a particular zone in the range making us feel that the scene is stabilised, which is actually a kind of deception because the camera isn’t actually moving here. But the Vivo X50 Pro definitely stands out in this region because it used the gimbal system even for videos. Also, you can pin a particular object and use this moving camera to track it without actually moving your mobile, which definitely takes a hit.

Well for a 600 USD starter, this mobile indeed offers much more for its price. Do we recommend it? Definitely yes, it comes with a matte black finish which doesn’t leave fingerprint traces giving you a premium feel. It not only is for the camera but also does a pretty good job at feel and performance, so it’s definitely worth buying.
