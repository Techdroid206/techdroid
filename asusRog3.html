ASUS ROG 3

Everytime Asus releases ROG mobile, it becomes the fastest mobile at that given point of time. It almost became a tradition at this point. Am I praising ROG series too much? Well latest ROG 3 is worth the praise.

The box itself comes with some unique design which definitely stands out from any other unboxing opening experience.we get aero active cooler 3 inside for gaming. We get 30W hyper charger with USB C to headphone jack adapter(which every company should probably provide if they're planning to neglect headphone jack) along with USB C cable and few rubber ports to seal the phone.

Alright, now for the phone itself, well its almost identical to it's previous generation with same identical substantial borders around the display.In the idea of making it look a bit cleaner, ROG 3 has lost its identity which is kind of a beast and aggressive when you look at previous version's body.Slanted buttons have become rounded buttons. Alright, these issues are negligible, you'll know why in a while.

What changes exactly did they brought here from ROG 2, well they have literally brought out the best of every possible component out there and placed it in here making it an absolute beast.

  • display has gone from 120 hz to 144 hz
  • ram has gone from 12 gigs to 16 gigs
  • storage speed ufs 3.0 to ufs 3.1.
  • the camera from 4k to 8k
  • the heat sink is 6 times larger now(so basically the cooler you get isn't actually needed, oh wait you might be needing an stand, so stick with it lol)
  • improved speakers

So basically they've improved everything which can't be seen but felt by actual usage. The speakers are so good that I won't hesitate to shout out that they are the best speakers attached to a smart phone right now.why smart phone's speakers are not that provided with deep base? To actually provide deep base the speaker have to pump lots of air which requires larger space, that's something smart phone practically can't do, so what asus has done here is they've come up with a software solution adding artificially extra base.And the haptics, the sensory experience you get out of it is just so good that you won't regret buying it even for a second

Alright,let's talk about the killer feature of this mobile - gaming, even a person who isn't into gaming have a more or less idea about how good asus ROG series is for gaming

Well there is a big debate out there regarding why every gaming phone's camera sucks, but this was changed with ROG 2 and it's completely vanished with ROG 3, the main camera here is an absolute beast, it even beats some of the modern day flagship in terms of picture crispness with pretty good details and you can take 8k videos at 30fps with stabilization(well, no kidding, this is the world's first smart phone that can do that)

ROG 3 is most powerful mobile out there in market right now with world's first 865+ QSD chipset which is pretty much 10% faster than already super faster 865 chipset and cooling has gotten really good (there is an air vent, there is a vapor chamber, there's a graphite film) it comes with 6000 mAh, which is enormous and acts as a power bank as well

Can you really take advantage of the 144hz display though? - when you play a game on pc you can adjust your frame rate according to your graphic card, but in Android the frame rate are locked by a particular game developer, so no matter how fast your mobile is it will still provide 60hz refresh rate, so we're not actually using that for gaming. So maybe the technology would mold in such a way that it'll set frame rate according to the mobile which is pretty much useful for a mobile like this.(maybe like MEMC where even 30 fps can be shown as something like 120 fps)

Do we recommend it? - people in general won't buy one mobile for gaming and the other one for camera, that's where ROG 3 comes into the scene providing both, I hope that helps with your concern.
